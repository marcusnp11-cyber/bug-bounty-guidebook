> In a world where digital threats are evolving faster than ever, the demand for skilled ethical hackers has never been higher. The Guidebook to The Fundamentals of Bug Bounty Hunting is your comprehensive entry point into the world of professional vulnerability research and responsible disclosure.

> This book walks you through the complete lifecycle of a bug bounty engagement — from selecting the right target and performing thorough reconnaissance, to discovering critical vulnerabilities and writing reports that get rewarded. Every chapter is packed with real-world scenarios, practical techniques, and actionable checklists.

> Whether you're a complete beginner curious about cybersecurity or a developer looking to understand the attacker's mindset, this guidebook bridges the gap between theory and practice. You'll learn the tools, methodologies, and thought processes used by professional ethical hackers on platforms like HackerOne, Bugcrowd, and Intigriti.

> By the final page, you won't just understand bug bounty hunting — you'll be ready to start your own journey, earn rewards, and contribute to a more secure internet for everyone.
